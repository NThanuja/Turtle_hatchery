Donation Options

Choose from the following donation options to support the turtle population:

  • Adopt a Turtle: Make a monthly donation to support the care and conservation of a specific turtle.
  • Protect Nesting Sites: Contribute towards protecting and monitoring nesting sites to ensure successful hatchings.
  • Education Programs: Support educational initiatives to raise awareness about turtle conservation among local communities and tourists.

Merchandise

Take home a piece of Sri Lanka and support turtle conservation with our merchandise:

  • Turtle T-Shirts: Show your love for turtles with our stylish and eco-friendly t-shirts.
  • Turtle Keychains: Carry a turtle-shaped keychain as a reminder of your commitment to conservation.
  • Turtle Souvenirs: Choose from a variety of turtle-themed souvenirs as a token of your visit.

How to Donate

To make a donation or purchase merchandise, please visit our online store or contact us:

Corporate Sponsorship

We also offer corporate sponsorship opportunities to organizations willing to contribute to turtle conservation:

  • Naming Rights: Have your organization's name associated with a specific turtle species or hatchery.
  • Brand Visibility: Gain brand exposure through our promotional materials and events.
  • Employee Engagement: Involve your employees in volunteering and awareness campaigns.

Volunteer Opportunities

Join our team of dedicated volunteers and actively participate in turtle conservation efforts:

  • Beach Monitoring: Help monitor nesting activity and protect nests from predators.
  • Hatchling Release: Assist with the release of baby turtles into the ocean.
  • Community Outreach: Educate local communities and tourists about turtle conservation.
